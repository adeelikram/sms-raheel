School Management System

The SMS School System has risen from its modest beginnings in 1975 as Les Anges Montessori Academy to become a major force in the education world. With an ever-expanding base,already established in UK, Malaysia, the Philippines, Pakistan, the UAE, Oman, Belgium and Thailand, SMS is one of the largest private school networks in the world.
